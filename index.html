Who Am I?

Hello! My name is Alyona Martinez, and I'm a 22 year old student with a newfound passion for computers. I studied studio art for my first few years of college, so this is far from my first time at El Camino- but I hope that this time, I can find a career path that suits me best. I have two sisters whom I love very much, with an extensive family in Europe and friends from all over the world. I consider myself an animal person, owning a dog, two cats and a ball python. My favorite food is udon.
